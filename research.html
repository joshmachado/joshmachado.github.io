Spatial Distribution and Clustering of GMCs

Every star begins its life embedded deep within the heart of a Giant Molecular Cloud (GMC). As the site of all star formation, GMCs are a fundamental building block of galaxies. The PHANGS-ALMA survey observed a sample of 98 nearby star-forming galaxies to catalog and study their star forming sites.

The PHANGS-ALMA survey identified more than 8,000 GMCs across their sample of galaxies. I am measuring the characteristic spacing of GMCs
